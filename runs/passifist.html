Introduction

The True Pacifist Route focuses on sparing every monster and building relationships instead of fighting. It leads to the most complete and peaceful ending.

  • Never kill monsters
  • Spare major bosses
  • Complete character friendships

Ruins & Snowdin

Learn the basics of mercy in the Ruins with Toriel, then move into Snowdin where you meet Papyrus and Sans.

  • Toriel: Spare her until she lets you leave.
  • Papyrus: Beat him by sparing and complete his date afterward.

Waterfall & Hotland

Undyne becomes your key character here. You must survive her chase and later befriend her. Hotland introduces Alphys and Mettaton’s show-based battles.

  • Undyne: Flee until she collapses, then give her water and befriend her.
  • Mettaton: Raise ratings instead of dealing damage.

You can also visit Tem Shop for useful items.

Core & True Lab

Complete Alphys’s date to unlock the True Lab and uncover the full story behind the Amalgamates.

  • Alphys: Complete her date event.
  • True Lab: Spare all Amalgamates using ACT commands.
  • Asgore: Fight until Mercy becomes available, then spare him.
  • Omega Flowey: Defeat then spare.
  • Asriel: Use SAVE mechanics to end the fight peacefully.

Ending

After completing all required friendships and story events, you achieve the True Pacifist Ending. The Underground is finally freed without violence.
