Undyne the Undying

Overview

Undyne the Undying is one of the hardest fights in the Genocide Route. She gains increased speed, damage, and aggressive spear patterns.

Recommended Items

  • Snowman Pieces
  • Cinnamon Buns
  • Crab Apples
  • Sea Tea

Armor / Weapon

  • Ballet Shoes (high damage)
  • Old Tutu (best defense for this fight)
  • Cloudy Glasses (extra survivability)

Fight Strategy

  1. Memorize spear directions instead of reacting blindly.
  2. Use small movements — overdodging gets you hit more.
  3. Heal early, not when you're already low.
  4. Focus on survival over aggression.

Key Pattern Tips

Green soul phases require precision timing. Yellow spear rings are about rhythm, not speed.
