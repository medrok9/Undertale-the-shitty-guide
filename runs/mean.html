Introduction

The Genocide Route is triggered by eliminating every monster in every region before progressing the story.

  • Clear every area's kill counter.
  • Encounters stop once an area is complete.
  • Missing required kills aborts the route.
  • Save points begin feeling colder and darker.

"But nobody came."

Ruins and Snowdin

Begin by fully clearing the Ruins before defeating Toriel. Continue grinding encounters until the game displays: "But nobody came."

Snowdin becomes empty and unsettling as NPCs disappear. Papyrus still believes you can change despite everything.

Waterfall

Waterfall introduces Undyne the Undying, one of the hardest bosses in the game.

Hotland and CORE

Hotland becomes almost completely abandoned. Mettaton NEO appears but is instantly defeated in one hit.

Sans begins directly judging your actions as the route progresses.
